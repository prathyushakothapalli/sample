PV SINDU

Pusarla Venkata Sindhu is an Indian badminton player. Considered one of India's most successful sportspersons, Sindhu has won medals at various tournaments such as the Olympics and on the BWF circuit, including a gold at the 2019 World Championships.In the case of PV Sindhu, the nickname "PVSindhu" has gained popularity due to her achievements and prominence as a professional badminton player.
